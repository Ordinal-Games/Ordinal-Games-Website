    • Welcome to Ordinal Games

    We are a group of 7 game developers who have a passion for video games and want to share the passion, Here at Ordinal Games we make games using Unity, C++ and SDL2, python and pygame, and we love pixel art. We are inspired to make games such as Hollow Knight, Shovel Knight, Celeste, and all these indie successes, We are currently developing a game called Fight Night a couch co-op game currently being made in Unity.

    Ordinal was founded by Luak and joined by Scoop, Harry, GameObject, Scriptline, XTOPY, SLIME, and The Captain Moo... Luak uses unity to make games, Scoop uses unity and c++/sdl2 and python/pygame, Scriptline uses unity and c++/sdl2 and python/pygame, Harry makes 3D Models and 3D art, Slime makes Pixel Art, XTOPY uses Unity, and Captain Moo uses Unity and FL Studio to make music
